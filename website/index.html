What Is LeetCode Automator and How Does It Work?

LeetCode Automator is an advanced Node.js script designed to simplify your LeetCode submission workflow. Whether you're maintaining your profile, bulk submitting solutions, or automating your coding practice, this tool provides:

  • Instant Automation: Fetch and submit solutions in seconds
  • Smart Filtering: Automatically skips premium-only & DB-only questions
  • Versatile Functionality: From GraphQL queries to direct submissions
  • 24/7 Availability: Always ready to assist you, anytime, anywhere
